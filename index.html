So you want to save the planet, but you don't know how?

Neither did we. That's why we built an app that helps you understand the carbon footprint of your food.

You know those nutrition labels that tell you how bad the stuff you're buying is for you? Those are great, but what if you wanted to know how bad the stuff you're buying is for the environment?

We believe that this data is important, and we want to give it to you. In comes Marble.

Cool! But how does it work?

Marble uses artificial intelligence to estimate the carbon footprint of pretty much anything you'd find in a grocery store. All you have to do is pick an item off the shelf and scan the barcode. Marble will handle the rest.

Scan a product barcode and add it to your shopping cart.

Manage the carbon footprint of your cart and checkout if everything looks good.

Set weekly goals and visualize your footprint by category, brand, and over time!
